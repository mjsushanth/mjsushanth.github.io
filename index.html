Text-to-Pose Diffusion
Built a CLIP-conditioned diffusion model with cross-attention and anatomical loss for 3D pose generation. Covers end-to-end motion/3D reasoning: pose representation, N-joint hierarchical mapping, kinematic chains, and pelvis–spine–extremity validation.
  • Architecture: Cross-modal CNN–Transformer UNet integrating CLIP embeddings with cross-attention at multiple resolution levels; hierarchical semantic conditioning for body-part context and action understanding.
  • 3D Reasoning: Biomechanical constraints including bone-length preservation, anatomical validation, and N-joint kinematic enforcement; dual-pass classifier-free guidance for sharper, text-faithful poses. Uses center-of-mass sanity checks to penalize physically implausible generations.
  • Engineering: Modular PyTorch pipeline, dynamic text–pose conditioning, dataset EDA with joint-mapping analysis, ablations over conditioning strength and noise schedules, pose visualization utilities for qualitative evaluation.
→ Design Documentation → Research Report
Multi-View 3D Scene Analysis
Built a Multi-view, Pose-guided correspondence system pipeline on ETH3D for scene understanding, pose-guided filtering, and correspondence analysis across cluttered views and occlusions.
  • Geometry Core: Camera pose estimation, local feature extraction, descriptor matching, and RANSAC-based geometric verification with epipolar constraints and inlier-ratio scoring.
  • Multi-View Reasoning: Pose-guided filtering, occlusion-aware masking, and match-quality scoring to promote correspondences that are consistent across multiple cameras, not just pairwise.
  • Evaluation & Tooling: ETH3D-driven experiments with reprojection-error diagnostics, per-scene match metrics, and structured HDF5/JSON scene manifests for reproducible runs and downstream analysis.
  • GPU-Accelerated Pipeline: PyTorch-based preprocessing with LAB color transforms, CLAHE enhancement, and bilateral filtering; achieved 56K+ features per image and ~28% spatial coverage with strong match quality.
