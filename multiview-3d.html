Multi-View 3D Scene Analysis on ETH3D

10k+ LOC pipeline for pose-guided multi-view feature correspondence on the ETH3D dataset. Integrates camera pose estimation, feature matching, occlusion handling, and multi-view consistency checks to build robust correspondences across cluttered scenes and varying baselines, with explicit tooling for diagnostics and reproducible experiments.
  • Pose-Guided Correspondence System: Combines camera pose estimation with view-overlap analysis to select candidate view pairs, then uses local feature extraction and descriptor matching to seed correspondences that are geometry-aware rather than purely appearance-based.
  • Geometric Core & Validation: Applies RANSAC-based geometric verification with epipolar constraints, inlier-ratio analysis, and per-pair reprojection statistics; integrates rejection stages for unstable matches and low-baseline configurations that are prone to degeneracies.
  • Multi-View Reasoning & Occlusion Handling: Promotes tracks that remain consistent across multiple cameras (not just pairwise) via match-quality scoring, track-formation logic, and occlusion-aware masking, allowing the pipeline to reason about visibility and scene layout.
  • GPU-Accelerated Preprocessing: Uses PyTorch to run LAB color-space transforms, CLAHE contrast enhancement, and bilateral filtering on the GPU; tuned to reach 56K+ features per image with ~28% spatial coverage and a 0.93 match-quality score on representative ETH3D scenes.
  • Experiment Infrastructure & Manifests: Organizes per-scene experiments into structured HDF5 and JSON manifests capturing camera intrinsics, poses, feature maps, and match statistics, enabling reproducible runs, ablation studies, and downstream 3D reconstruction or SLAM-style analyses.
